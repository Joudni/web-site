Personaly

Sasu is the leader of our family and the first cat we ever had.
He's always up to something mischievous and loves playing pranks on both humans and other cats in the house.
Sasu is a true lap cat and enjoys spending his days snuggled up with his favorite people.
But he's not just a cuddly kitty - Sasu is also the protector of the family and our very own king!
One of Sasu's favorite things to do is knock things off of tables and shelves, so we've had to rearrange our home decor to keep our things safe.
He also can't resist the temptation of butter, and whenever we make toast, Sasu will try to sneak a taste.
We've tried to keep him away from it, but he just won't give up!
