Onni cat born

Onni born in 11.12.2021.
Onni is a Bengal cat.
Onni tries to be the king of our family.

Personaly

Meet Onni, a playful bengal cat with a big appetite and a love for consistency!
He likes to have his alone time, but he also enjoys snuggling up with his owners to watch TV in the evenings.
Onni is best buds with Sasu, and he's very protective of Hilma when Sasu gets a little too playful.
Onni is serious about his meal times and likes to keep things consistent.
If his food isn't in the bowl by 4 pm, he'll let you know by tapping on his dishes.
He also loves to play with toys, and his favorite game is fetch - he'll chase after anything you throw and bring it right back like a little kitty retriever.
And when it comes to catnip, Onni just can't resist - it's his absolute favorite thing to play with!
With his playful spirit and love for routine, Onni is a true delight to have around.
